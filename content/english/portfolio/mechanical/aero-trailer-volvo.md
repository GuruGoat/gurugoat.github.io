## Overview

In conjunction with Volvo Australia, we upgraded a B-double combination to deliver [massive fuel savings.](https://www.trucksales.com.au/editorial/details/volvo-announces-world-first-fuel-super-truck-113998/)

## Features

The standard dual wheels were replaced with low rolling resistance "super single" wheels. This reduced rolling resistance and also gave us clearance to install panels outside the wheels. The composite panels streamlined the side of the trailer below the deck. To access underbody lockers and wheels, we implemented lift-up sections of panel with gas strut assist. We also fitted panels to the rear of the lead trailer's body to reduce the gap between the lead and tag trailers.
