## Overview

I joined the CFA as a volunteer firefighter about 13 years ago.

Since then I've had the experience a wide range of incidents including grass fires, house fires, vehicle accidents and even a ship fire! 

I'm in a busy brigade which responds to over 400 calls per year. Most of our work is around the Geelong area but we could be called anywhere in the state, particularly over summer.
